New Balance 990 - Dad Shoe Trendsetter

Written by SoleGrid Staff | April 20, 2025

Fashion

The New Balance 990 originally debuted in 1982 as one of the most advanced running shoes of its time, boasting a hefty price tag and unparalleled comfort. Over the decades, it evolved from a technical runner into a fashion-forward staple, embraced by dads, normcore fans, and celebrities alike.

Its transformation into a ‘cool dad shoe’ happened organically, thanks to its premium build quality, Made in USA craftsmanship, and no-frills aesthetic. From Silicon Valley entrepreneurs to supermodels off-duty, the 990 has become a surprising symbol of understated luxury and everyday wearability.

Now in its sixth iteration, the 990 continues to dominate. Each version retains the essence of the original while incorporating new tech and design cues. It's a masterclass in evolution—proof that practicality and style don’t have to be mutually exclusive in the sneaker world.
