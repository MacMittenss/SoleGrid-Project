Adidas Superstar - The Hip-Hop Icon

Written By SoleGrid Staff April 1, 2025
Fashion

Made famous by Run-DMC, the Adidas Superstar became a staple in streetwear culture. Known for its iconic shell toe and low-profile silhouette, the Superstar has transcended its roots in basketball to become a fashion essential. Its minimalistic design, often featuring bold stripes and clean colorways, makes it one of the most easily recognizable sneakers in history.

Throughout decades, the Superstar has been a symbol of rebellion and self-expression. Hip-hop culture adopted the shoe as a visual statement of authenticity and street credibility. Run-DMC famously wore them without laces, transforming the performance sneaker into a cultural icon. Adidas capitalized on this moment by signing a historic endorsement deal with the group — the first of its kind between a major brand and a musical act — sparking a new era of collaboration in fashion and music. Today, the Superstar remains a canvas for creative expression, embraced by both sneakerheads and the fashion elite.
