Nike Dunk Low - Skater’s Favorite

Written By SoleGrid Staff April 5, 2025
Sports

Originally designed as a basketball shoe, the Nike Dunk Low found new life within skateboarding culture during the late '90s and early 2000s. With its simple silhouette, durable build, and stylish colorways, it became a favorite among skaters and streetwear enthusiasts alike. The Dunk’s connection to skateboarding was further solidified with the introduction of the Nike SB line in 2002, which featured enhanced padding and grip ideal for skating.

Today, the Nike Dunk Low transcends its athletic origins, standing as one of the most sought-after models in sneaker history. Collaborative releases with brands, artists, and designers have driven enormous hype and high resale values. Its versatility — straddling sportswear, streetwear, and high fashion — has kept the Dunk Low at the forefront of sneaker culture across generations.
