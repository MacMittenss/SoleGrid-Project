Reebok Question - Iverson’s Legacy

Written by SoleGrid Staff | April 25, 2025

Sports

When Allen Iverson stepped onto the court in the Reebok Question in 1996, a legend was born. The shoe, with its bold toe cap and honeycomb cushioning, became synonymous with Iverson’s fearless style of play and rebellious attitude, capturing the imagination of a generation.

Beyond the court, the Question Mid became a cultural statement. Iverson’s impact reached far beyond basketball—he blurred the lines between athlete and icon, and his footwear followed suit. The silhouette became a staple in barbershops, music videos, and street fashion almost overnight.

Years later, the Reebok Question continues to see retro releases and collaborations that honor its heritage. From its Philly-inspired colorways to its leather and suede builds, this sneaker remains a fitting tribute to the player who redefined the game both stylistically and athletically.
