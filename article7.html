Nike Air Max 90 - Timeless Cushioning

Written By SoleGrid Team | March 2025
Tech

The Nike Air Max 90, originally released as the Air Max III in 1990, was designed by the legendary Tinker Hatfield. It was a revolutionary step forward in sneaker technology, introducing a larger visible Air unit and a sleek aerodynamic design that redefined comfort and performance.

What set the Air Max 90 apart was its bold aesthetic, particularly the iconic **Infrared colorway**, which made it instantly recognizable. The combination of mesh, leather, and suede materials gave it a futuristic look while maintaining durability and breathability. This sneaker wasn't just built for running—it became a streetwear essential.

Over the years, the Air Max 90 has been embraced by various subcultures, from hip-hop artists to skaters and even luxury fashion designers. It has been reimagined through collaborations with brands like **Off-White, Patta, and atmos**, proving its versatility and lasting appeal. The silhouette’s continued relevance is a testament to Nike’s ability to blend technology with culture.

Today, the Air Max 90 remains a staple in sneaker collections worldwide. Whether worn for performance or style, it stands as one of the greatest sneaker designs ever created, constantly evolving while staying true to its original roots.
